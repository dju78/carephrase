Privacy Notice

Published by Daramola Digital Labs  |  Last updated: June 2026

Summary CarePhrase has no user accounts and does not store your care notes. When you request AI coaching feedback, the text you have written is sent securely to our AI provider only to generate that feedback; it is processed transiently and is not retained by CarePhrase. You are reminded never to enter real service-user names or confidential personal information.

Who we are

CarePhrase is a product of Daramola Digital Labs, a UK-based digital tools provider. Our website is daramolajo.co.uk.

What data we collect

CarePhrase does not require an account and does not store your personal data. Specifically:

  • No account registration is required, and we do not ask for your name, email address, or contact details.
  • We do not store your care notes, practice content, or scores on our servers.
  • Your practice progress and supervisor scores are held only within your own browser and are not sent to our servers.
  • No cookies are used for tracking or advertising.

How AI coaching uses your text

CarePhrase coaches you on care notes you write yourself. To do this, when you press a button to request feedback, the note text you have entered is transmitted securely over HTTPS to our AI provider (Anthropic) solely to generate coaching feedback. This text is processed transiently to produce your feedback and is not stored by CarePhrase. We do not use it to build a profile of you or for advertising.

Because this text leaves your device, you must never enter real service-user names, NHS numbers, dates of birth, addresses, phone numbers, or any confidential personal information. Use a fictional name or write "the service user". A privacy reminder is shown at the point of entry on every screen.

Mock data only: Any names, scenarios, or records shown within CarePhrase are entirely fictional and created for training purposes. They do not represent real individuals.

Feedback form

CarePhrase includes an optional feedback link. If you choose to submit feedback, please be aware that the form is hosted by a third-party provider (Google Forms). Any data you submit via that form is governed by the privacy policy of that provider. We recommend you do not include personal information in feedback submissions.

Future versions

If a future version of CarePhrase introduces user accounts, server-side session saving, or new forms of data collection, this privacy notice will be updated before those features are released. Users will be informed of any material changes.

Your rights under UK GDPR

CarePhrase does not hold personal data about you on our servers, so there is no stored profile to access, correct, or delete. The note text sent for AI coaching is processed transiently and not retained. If a future version stores personal data, we will publish a full data processing record and your rights will apply in full under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Third-party services

CarePhrase uses Anthropic to provide AI coaching feedback and Google Forms to receive optional feedback submissions. It does not currently integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. If this changes, this notice will be updated accordingly.

Children

CarePhrase is designed for adult professional use. It is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to this notice

We may update this privacy notice from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of CarePhrase after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated notice.

Questions about privacy

If you have any questions about this privacy notice or how CarePhrase handles data, please contact Daramola Digital Labs.
