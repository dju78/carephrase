CareTalk UK — care-note training coach

by Daramola Digital Labs

CareTalk UK does not write the note for you. It trains you to write better notes.

What CareTalk UK is

CareTalk UK is a care-note training coach for Healthcare Assistants, Support Workers, Care Assistants and Nursing Assistants working in UK care settings. It is a free, mobile-friendly training app that helps care workers communicate professionally and confidently — from writing care notes to speaking up in emergencies — by teaching them to observe clearly, record factually, and escalate appropriately.

Care workers write their own care notes and receive coaching feedback on clarity, professionalism, completeness and safeguarding awareness, along with a reflective question and scores to help them improve.

What CareTalk UK is not

CareTalk UK is not a care-note generator and not a care-note rewriter. It does not produce a finished care note for staff to copy and paste, and it does not rewrite the care worker's sentences. The app is a coaching and learning tool: staff must always write their own care notes in their own words. CareTalk UK only provides guidance, identifies missing detail in plain English, and asks reflective questions so the care worker can improve their own writing.

Key features

  • Care Note Training Coach — write your own care note and receive coaching feedback on clarity, professionalism, completeness and safeguarding awareness. The app never rewrites your note for you.
  • Care-note training scenarios — practise the four-part CQC-aligned framework: what happened, how it happened, what you did, and whether escalation or safeguarding action was needed.
  • Handover Practice — practise giving clear, structured care handovers.
  • Care Vocabulary — learn key UK health and social care terms with examples.
  • Interview Practice — prepare strong answers for HCA and Support Worker interviews.
  • Safeguarding Language — learn how to report safeguarding concerns factually and professionally.
  • Emergency Call Practice — practise clear communication for 999, NHS 111, GP, nurse or manager calls.

Privacy and training disclaimer

Privacy: Do not enter real service-user names, NHS numbers, addresses, phone numbers or confidential personal information.

Training only: CareTalk UK supports learning and reflective practice. It is not legally certified or CQC-approved and is not a replacement for employer policy, clinical judgement, safeguarding procedures or professional supervision. Staff must write their own care notes and follow employer policy, care plans, safeguarding procedures and local reporting requirements.
